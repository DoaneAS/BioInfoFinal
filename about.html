Data sets

Yu-11: A dataset of ~1,200 binary interactions among proteins encoded by ~1,100 human genes, generated from systematic investigation using a Y2H screen (Yu et al Nat Methods 2011). Stitch-seq combines PCR stitching with next-generation sequencing, and increases the efficiency and cost effectiveness of Y2H screening.

HI-II-14: A data set of ~14,000 binary interactions from the second phase of the human interactome project (Rolland et al Cell 2014)

LIT-BM-13: Lit-BM-13: a high-quality binary literature dataset comprising ~11,000 protein-protein interactions that are binary and supported by at least two traceable pieces of evidence (publications and/or methods) (Rolland et al Cell 2014)
